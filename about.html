Kate's Life

Family, friends and school were Kate's life. She cherished her relationships with her parents, brothers, cousins, grandparents, aunts and uncles, her large extended family, and many friends. Kate always enjoyed going to school. She attended Hamilton and St. Francis Xavier Schools in Weymouth. She was a sophomore at Archbishop Williams High School in Braintree, MA where she excelled in academics and athletics, playing varsity volleyball, lacrosse, and track. Kate was also a member of the school chorus. She enjoyed country music and spending time on Cape Cod with family and friends. Kate's smile would light up the room. She will be remembered as beautiful, funny, thoughtful and kind. Beloved daughter of Robert G. and Nancy E. (Phalon) McCarthy. Loving sister of Connor N. and Liam G. McCarthy. Cherished granddaughter of Bill and Jean Phalon, Helen McCarthy and the late Gerry McCarthy, all of Braintree. Loving niece of Gayle and Dan OConnell; Bill and Daphne Phalon; Ellen and Mark Phillips; Susan and Sean Flanders; and Laurie and Brian Doherty. Loving cousin of Kimberleigh, Brendan, Johnny, Danielle, Amanda, Katelyn, Stephanie, Mark, Ryan, Jack, Cal, Molly, Maggie, Sean, Megan, Deanna, and Ava.

Kate's Impact

Kate reached so many different people during her young life. She was always there for her friends in times of need, went out of her way to sit with people eating all alone, and had an infectious smile that always changed the mood for the better. Kate was a very hard worker, extremely smart, and had a unique drive.
